The Best Movies According to Jimrey

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Fairy Tail: Dragon Cry

A sequel, a continuation of the main story of the animated series Fairy Tail.

Kung Fu Hustle

An Action/Comedy movie where a wannabe gangster aspires to join the notorious Axe Gang while residents of a housing complex exhibit extraordinary powers in defending their turf.

Weathering with You

It's a romantic fantasy movie with a sad ending where one of the main characters decided to do something to stop the disastrous weather.
